About us

IJOBUKKYgospelbata

This is ADEWUMI BUKOLA ESTHER , a Gospel Music Minister.
We are the African Praise Tabernacle (A.P.T.) Registered ®️ as IJOBUKKY Global Productions (I.G.P). with the federal government of Nigeria. Popularly known as IJOBUKKYgospelbata


She is from Osun State, Nigeria. She started singing at a very tender age. BUKOLA ADEWUMI is a multi - talented music minister. With ability to lead, lead-support and backup with professional touch who has featured along great artist across the country scaling from year 2009 till date. All major studios within and outside Osun state.


Her Stage Name IJOBUKKYgospelbata was coined from the Dynamism of her Energetic dance styles which resulted to ' BUKOLA the Dancer' IJOBUKKY (yes✅ ) though some misinterpret as Dance of BUKOLA🙃 {IJO BUKKY} (wrong❌).


She is a very gifted musician who can sing almost all parts. She studied Science Laboratory technology at Ladoke Akintola University (LAUTECH), Ogbomosho, Oyo State. Nigeria. class of '09.


BUKOLA ESTHER ADEWUMI Launched her Gospel Music band in 2009 Almost immediately she graduated along her visionary husband MICHAEL-CROWN ADEKUNLE ADEWUMI who is a lover of God, lover of music Who then had already registered as a member of Gospel Musician Association Of Nigeria (GOMAN) in response to God's calling and directive and was easier for the duo to get along in the work of the ministry. Our Anchor Scripture is 1Peter 2 : 9 which is so evident and practical in our lives.


BUKOLA ESTHER ADEWUMI has both official album and single tracks to her credit with several collaborations with other music ministers across the country. OBA AYINYINTAN is one of her album tittle.


She is also a pro in birthday skits and birthday surprises . A pro in handling Wedding Engagements (Alaga), Stage performer at any type of any moral functions, an MC, IJOBUKKY is the anchor of LETS RUB MINDS TOGETHER an online weekly program which talks about home affairs and general daily happenings of our daily life ( accessible through her social handles ... IJOBUKKYgospelbata) .

IJOBUKKY also owns a mini market with sales of all kinds of daily needs , foodstuffs selling at wholesales . (Location: Osogbo, Osun State.) Her ministry also owns a dancing school where she teaches , orientate and impact interested individuals in cultural moves .


IJOBUKKYgospelbata has many Awards to her credit. Among which are:

  • Osun Ultimate Award BEST GOSPEL MUSICIAN in Osun, Year 2019. Won through online votes.
  • HONORARY AWARD (2021) by St.JohnPaul Music.
  • AWARD OF EXCELLENCE (2022) by A-Sounds.
  • A LEGENDARY AWARD by Majestic Voices Of Heaven Int'l.
  • Royal Award Of Great Impact (2018) given by Royal Praises Prophetic Global Outreach.
  • Award Of Excellence (2020) by Christian Interdenominational Outreach (Joshua Awotunde).


Several Award Nominations like Open Celebrity Award (OCA) Yearly Nomination on Osun Ultimate Award (OUA).
IJOBUKKY has shared stage with many reknown Gospel Musicians including; Evang. Bukola Akinade ( Senwele Jesu), Dr. ASU Ekiye , Kenny black, SEGUN Obe, Kingsley Ike, Yinka Alaseyori, Dr. Buchi, AKINTUNDE Elijah (Olorun ko SoBe), Dr Yinka Ayefele, Mama Bola Are, Yetunde Are, Bisi Alawiye-Aluko, Dr Shola Allyson, Tope Olutokun, just to mention a few.


BUKOLA Esther ADEWUMI is happily married to MICHAEL-CROWN ADEWUMI. A member of the body of Fellowship Of Gospel Music Ministers Of Nigeria (FOGMMON) and the current Vice Chairman of FOGMMON Osun State. They are blessed with wonderful children.
